What is our vacation policy?

The vacation policy at emagine is as follows:

  • 0-2 years of service: 2 weeks of PTO
  • 3-9 years of service: 3 weeks of PTO
  • 10+ years of service: 4 weeks of PTO

Vacation time does not roll over, except for the first year of employment where a maximum of 40 hours can be rolled over. Unused accrued vacation time will be paid out upon termination.
